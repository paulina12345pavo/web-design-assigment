"A healthy nervous system is not always calm. It is flexible and resilient"

Ann Peach here! Welcome to nervous system regulation. My work is rooted in the knowledge that healing begins in the body. I have a background in somatic therapy modalities and a deep understanding of the nervous system. Whether you are here to seek support whilst navigating stress and challenging times or seeking ways to feel more grounded in yourself, I guide individuals through somatic bottom up practices that fosters embodied awareness, emotional regulation and return to inner safety. I'm so glad you are here. Take a look around to see if something resonates with you and I'd be honoured to be a part of and support you on your journey.
